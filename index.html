Welcome to
Awarenonymous
Providing free, online anonymous counseling for MCPS students

Summary:

Welcome to our platform, a safe and confidential space where you can seek the support and guidance you need for your mental well-being. We understand that life's challenges can be overwhelming, and sometimes, you just need someone to talk to. Our dedicated team of counselors is here to listen, assist, and empower you, all while preserving your anonymity. At our website, you can connect with a trained counselor through anonymous chat sessions. Whether you're dealing with stress, anxiety, depression, or simply seeking someone to talk to, our counselors are ready to provide a helping hand. Your privacy and security are of utmost importance to us, and you can chat with confidence, knowing that your personal information remains confidential. No matter what you're going through, you're not alone. Take the first step towards a healthier state of mind by starting an anonymous chat with one of our caring counselors. Your mental health journey begins here.

What We Offer:
Quick Chat:

We offer a quick chat service where users can open a completely anonymous chat with a licensed school counselor.

Contact Counselors:

We offer a contacting counselors service, where based on your school/location we can provide you with the contact information of counselors in your area.
