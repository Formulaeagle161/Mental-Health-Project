Resources
Here we provide a large list of resources for you to use

Suicide Prevention Resources:

National Suicide Prevention Hotline:

The national suicide prevention hotline (call or text 988) is open 24/7 and allows you to contact crisis counselors for emotional support and other services via web chat or call.

Crisis Text Line

The Crisis Text line allows you to text HOME to 741741 from anywhere in the United States, anytime. A live, trained Crisis Counselor receives the text and responds.

Montgomery County Crisis Hotline

Call 240-777-4000 - 24 hours/7 days/week to access Crisis services through: Montgomery County Crisis Center 1301 Piccard Drive Rockville, Maryland 20850. They offer Mobile Crisis Teams, Critical Incident Stress Management, and Residential Crisis Services.


Mental Health Resources

Tips For Coping With Stress

The CDC has complied a list of ways to help cope with stress. These include: taking breaks from watching, reading, or listening to news stories, taking care of yourself, talking to others and much more.

Treverlifeline for LGBTQ Youth

The Trevor Project has many resources for LGBTQ youth facing challenges. These include a hotline to text (678-678), to call (1-866-488-7386) and online chat
